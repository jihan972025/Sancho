Everything you need, in one app

Sancho combines AI chat, automation, trading, and communication into a single desktop experience.

💬

14 LLM Providers

OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, DeepSeek, Grok, Mistral, Perplexity, Qwen, and more. Switch models mid-conversation with streaming responses.

📱

Messenger Integration

Connect WhatsApp, Telegram, Slack, or Matrix. Chat with your AI agent from your phone, anywhere you go.

🌐

Browser Automation

@playwright/cli powered. Navigate websites, fill forms, extract data — all through natural language commands (48 actions).

📧

Email & Calendar

Search, read, and send emails via Gmail or Outlook. Create, list, and delete Google Calendar events.

📈

Crypto Trading

Real-time prices from Binance. Technical analysis with RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands. Auto-trading on Upbit with AI or rule-based strategies.

🎙

Voice Chat

Scan a QR code to voice-chat from your phone. Works on Wi-Fi or over the internet via Cloudflare Tunnel.

📁

File Manager

Browse, create, move, and organize files with natural language. AI-powered automatic sorting by file type.

⏰

Task Scheduler

Schedule recurring tasks with cron-like intervals. Get results delivered to your connected messenger automatically.
